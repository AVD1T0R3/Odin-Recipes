Homemade lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor — try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

ingredients

  • a pound of ground meat (½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef).
  • A diced onion is cooked until translucent with the ground meat.
  • a can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • chop two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic.
  • A dash of sugar
  • dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • lasagna noodles
  • cottage cheese and Parmesan. You'll also need shredded mozzarella.
  • Eggs, they act as a binding agent

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven (375 degrees F) for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
